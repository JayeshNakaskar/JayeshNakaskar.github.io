HELLO !

Hi, I’m Jayesh Nakaskar — a passionate programmer and software engineer with years of hands-on experience in the tech world.

Programming isn’t just a skill for me — it’s something I was born to do. Over the years, I’ve taught myself multiple languages including C, C++, C#, Java, Python, and many more. I thrive on logic-driven challenges and enjoy pushing the limits of what’s possible through code.

Beyond programming, I’ve developed expertise in various tech domains including Android development, Blockchain technology, Linux systems, and more.

For the past 4+ years, I’ve been deeply immersed in Unity, creating engaging and visually compelling games. I’ve built around a dozen games, gaining solid experience in game design, development, and deployment. I also have working knowledge of Unreal Engine, which allows me to expand my creative boundaries even further.

Lately, I’ve been exploring the intersection of crypto and gaming — a vision I’ve had for years. I’m passionate about building decentralized, rewarding, and player-driven gaming ecosystems by leveraging blockchain technologies.

If it involves tech, logic, or creativity — I’m all in.‌‌
